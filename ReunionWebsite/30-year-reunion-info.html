30-Year Reunion Information

Posted on May 7, 2015

Looking Back: 2015

Plans are finalized for this summer's Reunion, set for July 17-18, 2015 with all events in Independence.

30-Year Reunion Recap

What an incredible weekend we had celebrating 30 years since our graduation! Here's a look back at the events and memories from our 30-year reunion in 2015.

Event Schedule

Friday, July 17, 2015 - Welcome Reception

Location: Independence Country Club

Time: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Theme: "Back to the 80s"

The weekend kicked off with a fantastic welcome reception at the Independence Country Club. Classmates from near and far gathered to reconnect and reminisce about our high school days. The "Back to the 80s" theme was perfect - we had 80s music, decorations, and even some classmates sporting their best retro looks!

Saturday, July 18, 2015 - Main Celebration

Location: The Westin Kansas City at Crown Center

Time: 6:00 PM - Midnight

Activities: Dinner, Awards, Dancing

The main event was spectacular! Held at The Westin Kansas City at Crown Center, we enjoyed:

  • A delicious dinner with great food and wine
  • Special awards recognizing class achievements and milestones
  • A memorable slideshow of high school photos and memories
  • Dancing to the greatest hits from our era
  • Catch-up conversations that lasted late into the night

Highlights from the Weekend

Great Attendance

Over 120 classmates and guests attended the events, making it one of our most successful reunions yet!

Memorable Photos

Numerous photos were taken throughout the weekend, capturing the joy and reconnection of our class.

80s Music & Dancing

The dance floor was packed all night with our favorite 80s tunes and plenty of laughs and memories.

Awards & Recognition

We celebrated many achievements and milestones during our awards ceremony:

Special Awards

  • Farthest Distance Traveled: For classmates who came from the greatest distance
  • Most Changed: For the biggest transformation (in a good way!)
  • Most Successful: For outstanding professional achievements
  • Best Dressed: For the most creative 80s-themed outfit
  • Class Baby & Class Brain: Recognizing our youngest and most academically accomplished

Next Steps

Looking Ahead

After such a successful 30-year reunion, we're already planning for our upcoming milestones:

  • 35-Year Reunion (2020): Plans are in development
  • 40-Year Reunion (2025): Major celebration in the works!
  • 50-Year Reunion (2035): Golden anniversary - mark your calendars!
