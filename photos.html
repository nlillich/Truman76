Side Pockets - Lee's Summit

20-Year Reunion Photos

July 22-23, 2005

Fox & Hound & Hilton Garden Inn

WOW! What a great weekend of activities. And the photos prove it. Thanks to all who came out to join in the fun. On Friday, we prepared for 70-80 classmates and friends to get reacquainted at the Fox and Hound.

Friday Activities: Fox and Hound gathering
Saturday Activities: Golf outing, main celebration at Hilton Garden Inn
Highlights: Awards, slideshow, dancing, fond memories
