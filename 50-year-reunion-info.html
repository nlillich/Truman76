50-Year Reunion Information

Posted on September 29, 2025

Mark Your Calendars!

September 18-19, 2026 - Independence, Missouri

It's time for our 50-year reunion! We have a variety of events planned for September 18-19, 2026, so we hope you can find at least one opportunity to get together and celebrate this milestone. From golf and school tours to football games and our main celebration dinner, we've planned something for everyone!

Complete Reunion Details

Friday, September 18, 2026 - Golf Outing (Optional)

Adams Pointe Golf Club

Time: 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Location: 1601 NE RD Mize Rd, Blue Springs

Cost: $TBD per person

Format: Fun scramble - All skill levels welcome!

Join us for a casual golf outing in a fun scramble format—lighthearted, friendly, and just competitive enough to keep things interesting. Golf cart included.

Friday, September 18, 2026 - Truman High School Tour

Truman High School

Time: 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Location: Truman High School, Independence

Cost: Free

Come walk the halls of Truman—this time without worrying about being late to class! Take a tour and see all the exciting updates and changes made to the building. We're honored that the Truman High School Principal, Ronda Scott, has offered to host this special event.

Friday, September 18, 2026 - Football Game

Truman High School Stadium

Time: 7:00 PM

Location: Truman High School, Independence

Cost: Free for over 55

Game time is 7:00 p.m. Since we're all over 55, there's no entry fee! Come see the new facilities—football and all other sports (except swimming) are now played on this site. Please note: next year's football schedule is not yet fully confirmed, so this event is still tentative.

Friday, September 18, 2026 - Casual Social

Local Establishment

Time: 8:30 PM

Location: To Be Determined

Cost: TBD

Continue your reunion weekend with a relaxed get-together at a local establishment. This will be a wonderful opportunity to reconnect with old friends, share stories, and reminisce about our high school days.

Saturday, September 19, 2026 - Main Celebration

Adams Pointe Conference Center

Time: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Location: 1400 NE Coronado Drive, Blue Springs

Cost: $76 per person

Dress Code: Semi-formal to formal

The highlight of our reunion weekend! Join us for a formal dinner, awards ceremony, slideshow of memorable photos, and dancing to our favorite tunes from the 80s. We'll have special recognition for class milestones, reunions, and memorable achievements.

Event Schedule:
  • 6:00 PM: Cocktail hour and mingling
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner service
  • 8:00 PM: Awards ceremony and slideshow
  • 9:00 PM: Dancing and celebrations
  • 10:00 PM: Event concludes

Special Features

Awards Ceremony

Special recognition for class milestones, achievements, and memorable moments from our high school years.

Photo Slideshow

A nostalgic journey through our high school memories with photos from yearbooks, events, and past reunions.

80s Dance Party

Dance the night away to all your favorite 80s hits performed by our DJ. Don't miss this unforgettable experience!

Guest Information

We welcome spouses, significant others, and guests to join us for all events. Please indicate the number of guests when you RSVP so we can plan accordingly.

Guest Pricing

  • Golf Outing: Same as classmates ($TBD)
  • School Tour: Free
  • Football Game: Free
  • Casual Social: TBD
  • Main Celebration: Same as classmates ($76)

Accommodations

We've arranged a special room rate at the Courtyard by Marriott Kansas City East/Blue Springs for reunion attendees. The hotel is conveniently located and offers excellent amenities.

Reunion Hotel Block

  • Hotel: Courtyard by Marriott Kansas City East/Blue Springs
  • Rate: $134/night (plus tax)
  • Booking Deadline: August 21, 2026
  • Reservation: Call (816) 228-8100 and mention "THS Class of 1976 Reunion"
  • Amenities: Free breakfast, indoor pool, fitness center, business center

Transportation & Parking

All venues are conveniently located in the Independence/Blue Springs area. Free parking is available at all locations.

  • Adams Pointe Golf Club: Free parking at the clubhouse
  • Truman High School: Free parking in school lots
  • Adams Pointe Conference Center: Free parking at the venue
  • Courtyard by Marriott: Free parking for hotel guests
