40-Year Reunion Details

Posted on July 6, 2025

Event Schedule

Date: October 3-4, 2025

Location: Independence, Missouri

Friday, October 3, 2025

  • 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM: Casual Meet & Greet at Local Restaurant
  • Location: Fox & Hound Restaurant, Independence
  • Cost: $25 per person (includes appetizers and non-alcoholic beverages)

Saturday, October 4, 2025

  • 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Golf Outing (optional)
  • Location: Winterstone Golf Course, Independence
  • Cost: $50 per person (includes golf cart and lunch)
  • 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM: Main Reunion Celebration
  • Location: Hilton Garden Inn, Independence
  • Cost: $75 per person (includes dinner, dancing, and awards ceremony)

Event Details

Friday Night Meet & Greet

Start your reunion weekend with a casual gathering at the Fox & Hound. This is a great opportunity to reconnect with old friends, share stories, and reminisce about our high school days. Light appetizers and beverages will be available.

Saturday Morning Golf

Optional golf outing for those who enjoy the game. We'll have a scramble format to keep things fun and competitive. Lunch will be provided after the round.

Saturday Night Celebration

The main event of the weekend! Join us for a formal dinner, awards ceremony, slideshow of memorable photos, and dancing to our favorite tunes from the 80s. We'll have special recognition for class milestones, reunions, and memorable achievements.

Registration Information

To secure your spot and help us plan appropriately, please RSVP by August 15, 2025. Space is limited for each event, so early registration is recommended.

Registration Deadlines:

  • Early Bird (Discounted Rates): June 30, 2025
  • Regular Registration: August 15, 2025
  • Late Registration: September 15, 2025 (limited availability)

Accommodations

We've arranged special room rates at the Hilton Garden Inn for reunion attendees. Mention the "THS Class of 1976 Reunion" when booking.

  • Hotel: Hilton Garden Inn, Independence
  • Rate: $129/night (plus tax)
  • Booking Deadline: September 15, 2025
  • Contact: (555) 987-6543 - Ask for Reunion Block

What to Bring

  • Your favorite memories or photos to share
  • Comfortable shoes for dancing
  • Golf attire if participating in the golf outing
  • Camera to capture the memories
  • Name tag (we'll provide, but bring any customizations)

Contact Information

For questions or special accommodations, please contact:

  • Reunion Committee: reunion@sample-reunion.com
  • Phone: (555) 123-4567
