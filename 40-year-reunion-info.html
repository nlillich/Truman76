50-Year Reunion Information

Posted on September 29, 2025

Mark Your Calendars!

October 3-4, 2025 - Independence, Missouri

It's time for our 40-year reunion! We have a variety of events planned for October 3-4, 2025, so we hope you can find at least one opportunity to get together to reminisce about our amazing high school years.

RSVP Now Make Payment

Complete Reunion Details

Friday, October 3, 2025 - Meet & Greet

Fox & Hound Restaurant

Time: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Location: 123 Sample Avenue, Sample City, ST 12345

Cost: $25 per person (includes appetizers and non-alcoholic beverages)

Theme: Casual - Come as you are!

This is the perfect opportunity to reconnect with old friends in a relaxed atmosphere. We'll have plenty of appetizers, beverages, and space for conversation. Whether you can stay for just an hour or the whole evening, we'd love to see you!

Saturday, October 4, 2025 - Golf Outing (Optional)

Winterstone Golf Course

Time: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Tee time at 10:00 AM)

Location: 456 Sample Golf Way, Sample City, ST 12345

Cost: $50 per person (includes golf cart and lunch)

Format: Scramble - All skill levels welcome!

Join us for a fun morning on the links! We'll use a scramble format to keep things competitive and enjoyable for everyone. Lunch will be provided after the round. Golf clubs are available for rent if needed.

Saturday, October 4, 2025 - Main Celebration

Hilton Garden Inn

Time: 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Location: 789 Sample Hotel Boulevard, Sample City, ST 12345

Cost: $75 per person (includes dinner, awards, and dancing)

Dress Code: Semi-formal to formal

The highlight of our reunion weekend! We'll enjoy a delicious dinner, special awards ceremony, photo slideshow, and dancing to the greatest hits from the 80s. This is the event we've all been waiting for!

Event Schedule:
  • 6:00 PM: Cocktail hour and mingling
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner service
  • 8:00 PM: Awards ceremony and slideshow
  • 9:00 PM: Dancing and celebrations
  • 11:00 PM: Event concludes

Special Features

Awards Ceremony

Special recognition for class milestones, achievements, and memorable moments from our high school years.

Photo Slideshow

A nostalgic journey through our high school memories with photos from yearbooks, events, and past reunions.

80s Dance Party

Dance the night away to all your favorite 80s hits performed by our DJ. Don't miss this unforgettable experience!

Guest Information

We welcome spouses, significant others, and guests to join us for all events. Please indicate the number of guests when you RSVP so we can plan accordingly.

Guest Pricing

  • Friday Meet & Greet: Same as classmates ($25)
  • Saturday Golf: Same as classmates ($50)
  • Main Celebration: Same as classmates ($75)

Accommodations

We've arranged special room rates at the Hilton Garden Inn for reunion attendees. The hotel is conveniently located and offers excellent amenities.

Reunion Hotel Block

  • Hotel: Hilton Garden Inn, Independence
  • Rate: $129/night (plus tax)
  • Booking Deadline: September 15, 2025
  • Reservation: Call (555) 987-6543 and mention "THS Class of 1976 Reunion"
  • Amenities: Free breakfast, indoor pool, fitness center, business center

Transportation & Parking

All venues are conveniently located in Independence, MO. Free parking is available at all locations.

  • Fox & Hound: Ample free parking in the restaurant lot
  • Winterstone Golf Course: Free parking at the clubhouse
  • Hilton Garden Inn: Free parking for hotel guests
