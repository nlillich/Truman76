50-Year Reunion Info: September 2026

Posted on September 29, 2025

It's time for our 50-year reunion! We have exciting events planned for September 18-19, 2026, including golf at Adam's Pointe, a tour of Truman High School, football game viewing, and our main celebration dinner.

Event Highlights:

  • Friday, September 18: Golf outing, school tour, football game, and casual social gathering
  • Saturday, September 19: Main reunion celebration dinner at Adams Pointe Conference Center

Registration Deadline: August 15, 2026 (Early Bird rates available until June 30, 2026)

Space is limited for each event, so early registration is highly recommended. Join us for this special milestone celebration as we reconnect with classmates and create new memories together!
